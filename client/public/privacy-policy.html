Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Rupee Tracker ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your financial data remains secure. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our web application and Android mobile application.

Our app is designed to help you track personal expenses, manage budgets, and gain insights into your spending habits in a simple, secure way.

2. Data We Collect

To provide you with our services, we collect the following limited information:

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we store your username and email address to secure your data across devices.
  • Transaction Data: We store the expense details you manually enter, including amounts (in ₹), categories (e.g., Food, Travel), dates, and optional notes.
  • App Usage Data: Minimal anonymous analytics to help us improve app performance (e.g., crash reports).

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

Your security is our priority. We do NOT collect or store:

  • Bank Account Numbers or passwords.
  • Credit/Debit Card details (CVV, Expiry Date).
  • UPI PINs or Net Banking credentials.
  • SMS content or OTPs (One Time Passwords).
  • GPS Location data.

4. How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard protocols:

  • Database: All user data is stored in a secure, encrypted database (MongoDB).
  • Encryption: All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted via HTTPS (SSL/TLS).
  • Password Security: User passwords are hashed using strong encryption (Bcrypt) before storage; we cannot read your password.

5. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely for the purpose of the application:

  • To display your expense history and dashboard.
  • To generate visual charts and reports of your spending.
  • To calculate budget limits and savings forecasts.
  • To authenticate your login securely.

6. Data Sharing Policy

We have a strict No-Selling policy:

  • We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.
  • We do not share your financial data with third-party advertisers or marketing agencies.
  • Data may only be disclosed if required by Indian law or legal process.

7. User Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: You can view all your stored data within the app at any time.
  • Deletion: You can request full deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting support. Once deleted, data cannot be recovered.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal data and password.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
